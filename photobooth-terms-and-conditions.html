Photo Booth Text Messaging Service

Last updated: March 2026

Program Name

Rahaman Events Co. Photo Booth Text Messaging Service

Program Description

Rahaman Events Co. operates an opt-in SMS service that allows event guests to receive digital copies of their photo booth session via text message. Guests voluntarily provide their mobile phone number when prompted by the photo booth kiosk or booth attendant at a live event to receive their photos directly to their phone.

  • SMS messages are sent per session during the event only.
  • Guests will not be contacted after the event concludes, with one exception:
  • If the event organizer requests a digital photo album, one additional message containing a link to the album may be sent to all opted-in guests as a final communication closing out the event. No further messages will be sent after that.

By providing your phone number at the photo booth, you consent to receive SMS messages from Rahaman Events Co. (929-274-4714) containing your event photos and, if applicable, a link to a final event photo album.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies by session. You will typically receive 1–2 messages per photo booth session. If an event organizer requests a digital photo album distributed after the event, you may receive 1 additional message after the event concludes. You will not receive any messages beyond those described above.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Charges are determined by your wireless carrier and your mobile plan. Rahaman Events Co. does not charge any fees for this service. Contact your wireless carrier if you have questions about your messaging plan.

How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance. You may also contact us directly:

  • Email: info@rahamaneventsco.com
  • Phone / Text: 929-274-4714
  • Website: rahamaneventsco.com

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message at any time to unsubscribe from this messaging program. After texting STOP, you will receive a one-time confirmation message and will not receive any further SMS messages from Rahaman Events Co. through this program.

Supported Carriers

Our messaging service is available through most major U.S. wireless carriers. Not all carriers are supported. Message and data rates may vary depending on your carrier and plan.

Privacy

Your phone number will never be shared with third parties or used for marketing purposes. It is used solely to deliver the photo content you requested. For full details, please review our Photo Booth Privacy Policy.
