Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to all inquiries within 2 hours during business hours. Evening and weekend inquiries are typically answered the same day or by the next morning.

Do you require a deposit to book?

Yes, a deposit is required to secure your date. The amount and payment terms will be clearly outlined in your contract.

Can I see a sample contract before booking?

Absolutely! We're happy to provide a sample contract so you know exactly what to expect. Just ask when you contact us.

What if I need to change my event date?

We work with clients to accommodate date changes when possible. Details are in the contract, but we aim to be as flexible as we can.

Do you travel outside your service area?

We primarily serve NYC Metro, but we may be able to accommodate events outside our standard area. Contact us to discuss your location.
