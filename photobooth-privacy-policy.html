Privacy Policy

Photo Booth Text Messaging Service

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Rahaman Events Co. ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our photo booth SMS text messaging service. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Summary: Your phone number is used only to send you your event photos via text. It will never be shared with anyone or used for marketing. Period.

Information We Collect

Phone Numbers

When you choose to receive digital copies of your photo booth session via SMS, you voluntarily provide your mobile phone number to the photo booth kiosk or our booth attendant. This is entirely optional — you are not required to provide your number to use the photo booth.

Event Photos

Images are captured by our photo booth equipment during your session at the event. Photos are taken with your participation and knowledge at a live event.

How We Use Your Information

Phone Numbers

Your phone number is used only for the following purposes:

  • To send you digital copies of your photo booth session via SMS during the event.
  • If the event organizer requests a digital photo album, to send you one final SMS message containing a link to the album after the event concludes. This is the last message you will ever receive from us through this program.

Your phone number will never be used for marketing, promotional, or advertising purposes of any kind. We will not send you unsolicited messages. We will not contact you after the event beyond the delivery of your photos and, if applicable, a final event album link.

Event Photos

Images taken at our photo booth events may be used by Rahaman Events Co. for marketing purposes, including but not limited to our website, social media accounts, and promotional materials.

This is an opt-out practice. If you do not wish for your images to be used for marketing purposes, please contact us at info@rahamaneventsco.com or 929-274-4714 and we will honor your request promptly.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your phone number with any third party for any reason. Your mobile number is not shared with the event organizer, any partner, advertiser, or any other entity. It is held solely by Rahaman Events Co. and used exclusively to deliver the photo content described in this policy.

Data Retention

Phone numbers collected through our photo booth service are not retained beyond the period necessary to deliver your photos and, if applicable, a final event album message. Once delivery is complete, your number is not stored in any active marketing or contact database.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of SMS: Reply STOP to any text message you receive from us. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
  • Opt out of photo marketing use: Contact us at info@rahamaneventsco.com or 929-274-4714.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Your phone number is not stored in publicly accessible systems.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
