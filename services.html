Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for popular dates (weekends, holidays). However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

What areas do you serve?

We serve New York City, Nassau County, Suffolk County, and Queens. Contact us if you're unsure whether your location is covered.

Do you charge travel fees?

For photo booth rentals, travel is included within our standard service area. For table and chair rentals, delivery fees may apply based on distance and quantity.

What if I need the booth for longer than 4 hours?

We can absolutely extend your rental! Contact us to discuss pricing for additional hours.

What is your cancellation policy?

Details are in our contract, but we work with clients to be as flexible as possible. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.

Can I see the equipment before booking?

Yes! We're happy to arrange a time for you to see our equipment. Just reach out to schedule.

Are you insured?

Yes, we carry $1 million in general liability insurance for your peace of mind and venue requirements.
